# Nutrition

A Minecraft plugin for [Paper](https://papermc.io/), where players have to maintain a **balanced diet** in order to stay healthy and gain advantages.

---

## Description

With this plugin, food is no longer just a hunger bar filler.  
Players need to eat a variety of foods (fruits, vegetables, proteins, carbs, etc.) to keep their diet balanced.  
Unbalanced eating habits can lead to negative effects, while balanced diets bring positive bonuses.

---

## Features

- Tracks what kind of food the player eats.
- Requires a **balanced mix** of food categories.
- Rewards players for eating healthy and varied.
- Punishes monotone or unbalanced diets (e.g. always eating only bread).
- Designed for Paper servers.
- Written in Kotlin.
